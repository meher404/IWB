About Us

What we are

We the software engineers of IIIT-H came up with an innovative idea to provide a common platform for the people with similar thoughts. We have developed a browser plug-in, which facilitates user to chat with people who are browsing for similar topics. It is the best tool for knowledge sharing in this Information era. The main motive is to encourage the young talent to think loud by giving an exposure to group interaction which includes people from different parts of world with various ideas
